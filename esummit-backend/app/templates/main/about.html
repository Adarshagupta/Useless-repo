About ESummit

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind ESummit

Our Mission

ESummit is dedicated to fostering innovation and entrepreneurship among students and professionals. We aim to create a platform where ideas can flourish, connections can be made, and skills can be developed through various events, hackathons, and workshops.

Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech leaders and innovators by providing them with the resources, knowledge, and network they need to succeed in the ever-evolving technology landscape.

Our Vision

We envision a world where innovation is accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or resources. We strive to bridge the gap between academia and industry by creating a vibrant ecosystem where students, professionals, and industry leaders can interact and collaborate.

By 2030, we aim to become the leading platform for tech events and hackathons, recognized globally for our impact on fostering innovation and nurturing talent.

Our History

Founded in 2018, ESummit started as a small campus event aimed at bringing together technology enthusiasts. What began with just 50 participants has now grown into a major tech event platform with thousands of participants from across the globe.

Over the years, we have organized over 100 events, including hackathons, workshops, seminars, and networking sessions. Our events have attracted top-tier speakers from companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and many successful startups.
