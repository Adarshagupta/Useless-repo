ESummit Registration Instructions

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for registering for different types of events on the ESummit platform.

Registering for Individual Events

Step 1: Browse Events

Navigate to the Events page to see all available events.

Step 2: View Event Details

Click on an event to view its details, including description, date, venue, and registration deadline.

Step 3: Register for the Event

Click the "Register" button on the event details page and complete any required information.

Step 4: Confirmation

After successful registration, you'll receive a confirmation and the event will appear in your dashboard.

Registering for Team Events

Important!

For team events, you must first create or join a team before you can register for the event.

Option 1: Create a New Team
  1. Visit the event details page
  2. Click "Create New Team" button
  3. Enter your team name
  4. Add team members' emails (optional)
  5. Submit the form
Option 2: Join an Existing Team
  1. Visit the event details page
  2. Click "Join Existing Team" button
  3. Enter the team code (ask your team leader)
  4. Submit the form
Managing Your Team

As a team leader, you can add or remove members from your team page in the dashboard.

Registering for Hackathons

Two-Step Registration Required!

For hackathons, team creation is only the first step. After creating a team, you must also complete the hackathon project registration as explained below.

Step 1: Create or Join a Team

Follow the same steps as for team events to create or join a team.

Step 2: Team Formation

Make sure all your team members have joined the team. Team size must be within the limits specified for the hackathon.

Step 3: Complete Hackathon Registration (REQUIRED)

This step is critical and can only be done by the team leader:

  1. Go to your team details page
  2. Click "Register for Hackathon" button
  3. Fill in your project details:
    • Project name
    • Project description
    • Technologies you'll use
    • GitHub repository (if available)
    • Any other relevant links
  4. Submit the registration form

Without completing this step, your team is NOT officially registered for the hackathon!

Step 4: Confirmation

After successful registration, your project details will appear on your team page, and all team members will be officially registered for the hackathon.
